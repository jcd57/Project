Warframe

Warframe is a third person, looter shooter rpg developed by Digital Extremes.

The games takes place in an ever changing universe, where the players use warframes and weapons to fight multiple factions and bring balance bag to the galaxy.

The game features CO-OP options with a very welcoming community.
